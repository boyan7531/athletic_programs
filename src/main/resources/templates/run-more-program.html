My Run More Program

Welcome to your personalized Run More Program! Follow the schedule and guidelines below to increase your stamina and running performance.

Weekly Schedule

Week 1-8

  • Monday: Long run - Aim for 5-10 km
  • Tuesday: Cross-training - Swimming or cycling
  • Wednesday: Rest day
  • Thursday: Interval training - 5 x 400m sprints with 2-minute rest
  • Friday: Strength training - Focus on core and leg exercises
  • Saturday: Easy run - 3-5 km at a comfortable pace
  • Sunday: Rest day

Exercise Details

Running Workouts

Running workouts are essential for building stamina and speed. Follow the schedule for long runs, interval training, and easy runs to improve your performance.

Cross-Training

Incorporate cross-training activities like swimming or cycling to give your running muscles a break and improve overall fitness.

Strength Training

Strength training helps build muscle and prevent injuries. Focus on core and leg exercises such as planks, squats, and lunges.

Nutrition Plan

Fuel your body for optimal performance with a balanced diet. Here are some guidelines:

  • Eat plenty of carbohydrates for energy
  • Include lean proteins for muscle repair
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Limit sugary drinks and snacks

Track Your Progress

Use the following tools to track your progress:

  • Weekly running logs and distance tracking
  • Keep a workout and nutrition diary
  • Set short-term and long-term goals
  • Join our online community for support and motivation
Tip: Consistency is key! Keep pushing even if progress seems slow.
